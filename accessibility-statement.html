Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to an accessible and inclusive web experience.

Our Commitment

Barrett Financial Group, LLC and Robert Hendley are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We believe that every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, comfort, and independence — and that commitment extends to how we design and maintain this website.

We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide an equal experience for all visitors.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities. Conformance with these guidelines helps make the web more user-friendly for all people.

Accessibility Features

This website incorporates the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure for compatibility with screen readers
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Descriptive alt text on images
  • Keyboard-navigable interface elements
  • Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels
  • Clear, consistent navigation across all pages
  • Form labels and error messages for assistive technologies

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content on this website may not yet fully meet the strictest accessibility standards. We are actively working to identify and address any gaps. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us so we can assist you directly and work to resolve the issue.

We're Here to Help

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this website, or if you need information in an alternative format, please reach out to us during business hours. We will make every effort to provide the information you need in a way that is accessible to you.
