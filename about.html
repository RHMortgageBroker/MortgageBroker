A Broker Who Puts You First

I'm Robert Hendley, a licensed mortgage broker with Barrett Financial Group. My work is built on one simple principle: helping real people navigate one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives with confidence and clarity.

I take the time to understand your unique financial situation, explain your options in plain language, and work tirelessly to find the right loan — not just any loan. Whether you're purchasing your first home, moving up, investing, or refinancing, I'm in your corner from application to closing.

With access to 160+ lender partners through Barrett Financial Group, we have the tools to find the most competitive rates and the right loan for your life — not a one-size-fits-all approach. Lower interest rates mean lower monthly payments and more savings over the life of your loan, and we work hard every time to secure the best rate available for your specific situation.
